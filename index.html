About MIT

Madras Institute of Technology - A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1949, Madras Institute of Technology (MIT) is a pioneering engineering institute in India, located in Chromepet, Chennai. It is renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities, housing over 3,000 students across various disciplines. MIT boasts a distinguished faculty with expertise in engineering and technology, leading research in fields like aerospace, robotics, and more. Notable alumni include Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, a testament to MIT's legacy of excellence.

About Dept of Instrumentation Engineering

Excellence in Instrumentation and Control

Established in 1960, the Department of Instrumentation Engineering at MIT specializes in instrumentation and control systems. With advanced labs for automation and over 200 students engaged in cutting-edge projects, the department fosters innovation in IoT, robotics, and process control. Its experienced faculty, with deep industry and research expertise, guide students to excel. The department proudly hosts Intecho, a national-level technical symposium, showcasing its commitment to technical excellence.
